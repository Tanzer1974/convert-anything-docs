How do I use the extension?
Simply select any text containing measurements on any webpage, right-click, and choose "Convert Anything: Convert Units" from the context menu. The extension will instantly detect the unit and show you conversions.
What measurement categories are supported?
Our extension supports 16 comprehensive categories including Length, Temperature, Weight, Area, Volume, Time, Pressure, Energy, Currency, and more - totaling 200+ individual units.
Does the extension work with different number formats?
Yes! We support both European (1.200) and US (1,200) number formats, so the extension works seamlessly regardless of your location or preference.
Is my data private and secure?
Absolutely! All conversions are processed locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Your privacy is our priority.
The extension isn't working. What should I do?
First, try refreshing the page and ensuring the extension is enabled. If the issue persists, please contact us via email with details about the problem.
